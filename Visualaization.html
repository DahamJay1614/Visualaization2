Global Terrorism: A Data-Driven Analysis

This article explores global terrorism data through interactive visualizations, providing insights into patterns and trends in terrorist attacks over the years. The visualizations focus on various aspects such as global attack distribution, attack types, and terrorism impacts in specific regions like Malaysia.

Global Distribution of Terrorist Attacks

The map below displays terrorist attacks across the world. Countries are shaded based on the number of attacks they experienced in the selected year, with darker red indicating higher numbers of attacks. Use the year selector to explore how global terrorism patterns have changed over time.

Types of Terrorist Attacks

This bar chart breaks down the types of terrorist attacks, allowing you to select a country and year to see the relative frequencies of attack types such as bombings, armed assaults, and kidnappings. The chart provides insight into the diverse methods used in terrorism globally.

Terrorist Attacks Over Time

The bubble chart shows the number of terrorist attacks in selected countries over a span of years. The size of each bubble represents the number of attacks in a given year, helping you identify periods of increased or decreased terrorist activity.

Terrorism in Malaysia: Attacks and Deaths

The trend line and bar chart below focus on Malaysia, showing the number of terrorist attacks and related deaths over recent years. This visualization highlights the trends in terrorism activity within Malaysia, offering a closer look at the specific impacts of terrorism in the country.
